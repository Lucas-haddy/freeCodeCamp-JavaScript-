Simple Pizza Recipe

This simple pizza recipe is a favorite of my kitchen menu, its simple and easy to make with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500g Flour (any kind)
  • 1 1/2 tsp Instant Dry Yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 50 ml oil
  • 300 ml warm water

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, yeast and salt. Add the water and oil and mix the dough with a mixer or by hand for 5 minutes.
  2. Tip the dough on a lightly floured surface and knead into a ball until smooth.
  3. Put dough into an oiled bowl and leave to rise for 1-2 hours.
  4. Divide the dough into between 2-6 pieces and roll into balls, then leave it for another 10 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough thinly until its relatively 2mm thin. Put onto an oiled baking tray and cover with toppings.
  6. Put the pizza's into the oven at 180*C and bake for 15-30 minutes until puffy and golden.
